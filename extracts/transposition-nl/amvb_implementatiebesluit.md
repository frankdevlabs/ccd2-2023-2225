The AMvB amends the **Besluit Gedragstoezicht financiële ondernemingen Wft (BGfo)**, the **Besluit
kredietvergoeding** and the **Besluit bestuurlijke boetes financiële sector**. It is intended to enter into
force together with the Implementatiewet (Kamerstuk 36924).

## <a id="bgfo-advertising"></a>BGfo — advertising prohibitions *(draft)*

Four new advertising prohibitions are introduced in the BGfo, including:
- a ban on advertising suggesting that a credit **improves** the consumer's financial situation;
- a ban on advertising that a **BKR registration** has little or no effect on a credit application.

(The full list of four prohibitions is in the draft BGfo articles — verify against the final text.)

## <a id="bgfo-creditworthiness"></a>BGfo — creditworthiness & database thresholds *(draft)*

- **Open verification norm** — abolition of the **€1,000** verification threshold (art 113 BGfo).
- Removal of the **€250 BKR-consultation threshold** so BKR must be consulted for **every** consumer-credit
  agreement (art 114 BGfo).
- **Annual re-assessment** for revolving credit (doorlopend krediet).

## <a id="besluit-kredietvergoeding"></a>Besluit kredietvergoeding — cost regime *(draft)*

- A specific cost regime / cap for **deferred-payment credit** (BNPL and cards), including a maximum
  **late-payment fee** (new art 12 Besluit kredietvergoeding — chargeable 14 days after notice of default;
  reported max **€20 per notice and €60 per calendar year**, per Loyens & Loeff — *secondary, verify*).
- The maximale kredietvergoeding (APR cap) regime continues via the Besluit kredietvergoeding.
