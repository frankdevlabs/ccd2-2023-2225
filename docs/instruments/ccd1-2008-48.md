# CCD1 — Directive 2008/48/EC (repealed by CCD2)

> The instrument CCD2 replaces. Listed in [`tracker.yaml`](../../tracker.yaml) `instruments` and
> `keep_apart` (its own legislative process is **not** tracked here).

- **Full title:** Directive 2008/48/EC on credit agreements for consumers.
- **Repeal:** repealed by Directive (EU) 2023/2225 (CCD2) **with effect from 20 November 2026** (CCD2 art
  47). CCD1 continues to apply to credit agreements existing on 20 Nov 2026 until their termination,
  **except** that CCD2 arts 23, 24, 25(1) 2nd sentence, 25(2), 28 and 39 apply to all open-end credit
  agreements existing on that date.
- **Scope baseline (what CCD2 changes):** CCD1 covered agreements of **€200–€75,000**; CCD2 abolishes the
  €200 floor and raises the ceiling to €100,000 — see [scope](../provisions/scope-bnpl-cards-overdraft.md).

This page exists so the "current law → CCD2" lane in [`../what-changed.md`](../what-changed.md) has a
stable anchor for the CCD1 baseline.
