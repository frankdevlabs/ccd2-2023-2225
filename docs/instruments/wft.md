# Wet op het financieel toezicht (Wft) — amended by the Implementatiewet

> The principal national law the Dutch transposition amends. Listed in [`tracker.yaml`](../../tracker.yaml)
> `instruments`.

The Implementatiewet (Kamerstuk 36924) and the Implementatiebesluit amend the Wft. Key new/changed
provisions tracked in [`../provisions/`](../provisions/):

| Provision | Change | Page |
|---|---|---|
| Art 1:1 | "Verlenen van uitstel van betaling" added to the definition of credit | [scope](../provisions/scope-bnpl-cards-overdraft.md) |
| Art 1:20 (+ new 1:20(4)) | Exceptions removed; narrow ≤50-day BNPL carve-out; deferred-debit cards +6 months | [scope](../provisions/scope-bnpl-cards-overdraft.md) · [deferred-debit cards](../provisions/deferred-debit-cards.md) |
| Art 2:60 / 2:80 / 2:81 | Licence / intermediary / lighter-registration regimes | [scope](../provisions/scope-bnpl-cards-overdraft.md) |
| Art 4:34 | Creditworthiness test recast ("noodzakelijk en evenredig") | [creditworthiness](../provisions/creditworthiness.md) |
| New art 4:34a | Bans social-network & special-category data in the assessment | [creditworthiness](../provisions/creditworthiness.md) |
| New art 4:34b | Public-law ban on credit to minors + age verification | [minors ban](../provisions/minors-ban.md) |
| New art 4:35b | Early detection of payment problems + referral to debt advice | [arrears](../provisions/arrears-forbearance.md) |
| Art 1:77q | AFM product-intervention powers | [supervision](../transposition/supervision-afm.md) |

The AMvB additionally amends the **BGfo** (arts 113-114, advertising), the **Besluit kredietvergoeding**
(late-payment cap, maximale kredietvergoeding) and the **Besluit bestuurlijke boetes financiële sector** —
see [`../transposition/implementatiebesluit.md`](../transposition/implementatiebesluit.md).
