# Wet handhaving consumentenbescherming — amended by the Implementatiewet

> One of the four national laws the Implementatiewet (Kamerstuk 36924) amends, alongside the
> [Wft](wft.md), [BW Boek 7](bw-boek7.md) and the Overgangswet nieuw Burgerlijk Wetboek. Listed in
> [`tracker.yaml`](../../tracker.yaml) `instruments`.

The amendment aligns enforcement-of-consumer-protection competences with the new CCD2 consumer-credit
regime. Detail to be filled from the MvT (36924-3) when transcribing the operative text into
[`../../extracts/transposition-nl/`](../../extracts/transposition-nl/).
