# What changed — provision-by-provision

> The authority on what CCD2 changes. **Directive adaptation:** the three lanes are **current NL/CCD1 law
> → CCD2 (the directive) → Dutch implementation (bill 36924 / AMvB)**, so it is clear what the *directive*
> requires versus how the *Netherlands* implements it. Sections follow the **directive's own chapter
> structure** (Ch I–XV). Each provision cell links to its [`provisions/`](provisions/) page, which
> deep-links the operative extracts ([`directive/`](../extracts/directive/) — verbatim CCD2;
> [`transposition-nl/`](../extracts/transposition-nl/) — verbatim bill 36924). Working analysis from the
> MvT (36924-3) + draft AMvB, supplemented by law-firm briefings (labelled *secondary*) — verify against
> the Staatsblad text.
>
> This is the full table; [`STATUS.md`](../STATUS.md) carries a high-salience headline subset. Positions
> data: [`data/positions.csv`](../data/positions.csv).

## Chapter I — General provisions & scope

| Provision | Current law → CCD2 directive | Dutch implementation (36924 / AMvB) |
|---|---|---|
| [**Scope & thresholds**](provisions/scope-bnpl-cards-overdraft.md) | *CCD1:* €200–€75,000 band; BNPL / short-term credit largely excluded. **CCD2 (arts 2, 4):** €200 floor abolished, ceiling €100,000; BNPL, cards, authorised overdrafts in scope | "Verlenen van uitstel van betaling" added to credit ([Wft 1:1](../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-I-wft.md#wft-1-1)); [art 1:20](../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-I-wft.md#wft-1-20) exceptions narrowed; AFM licence (2:60) / intermediary (2:80) required; ≤50-day cost-free BNPL exception; lighter regime for large suppliers ([2:81](../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-I-wft.md#wft-2-81)) *(secondary)* |
| [**Ban on credit to minors**](provisions/minors-ban.md) | *Current:* no specific ban. **CCD2 (art 6):** objective-justified [age differentiation](../extracts/directive/general-provisions.md#article-6) permitted | **New [Wft 4:34b](../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-I-wft.md#wft-4-34b)** public-law ban + age verification; **[BW 7:75(4)](../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-II-bw7.md#bw-7-75)** makes agreements with minors *voidable* |
| [**Deferred-debit cards**](provisions/deferred-debit-cards.md) | *Current:* out of scope. **CCD2:** Member-State option to exempt | NL does not exempt but applies the rules **6 months later** ([inwerkingtreding](../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_slotbepalingen.md#inwerkingtreding)) |
