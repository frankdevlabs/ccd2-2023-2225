# What changed — provision-by-provision

> The authority on what CCD2 changes. **Directive adaptation:** the three lanes are **current NL/CCD1 law
> → CCD2 (the directive) → Dutch implementation (bill 36924 / AMvB)**, so it is clear what the *directive*
> requires versus how the *Netherlands* implements it. Each provision cell links to its
> [`provisions/`](provisions/) page; substantive cells will deep-link into the operative extracts once
> transcribed ([`directive/`](../extracts/directive/), [`transposition-nl/`](../extracts/transposition-nl/)).
> Working analysis from the MvT (36924-3) + draft AMvB, supplemented by law-firm briefings (labelled
> *secondary*) — verify against the Staatsblad text.

## Scope & licensing

| Provision | Current law → CCD2 directive | Dutch implementation (36924 / AMvB) |
|---|---|---|
| [**Scope & thresholds**](provisions/scope-bnpl-cards-overdraft.md) | *CCD1:* €200–€75,000 band; BNPL / short-term credit largely excluded. **CCD2:** €200 floor abolished, ceiling €100,000; BNPL, cards, authorised overdrafts in scope | "Verlenen van uitstel van betaling" added to credit (Wft 1:1); art 1:20 exceptions removed; AFM licence (Wft 2:60) / intermediary (2:80) required; narrow ≤50-day cost-free BNPL exception in new Wft 1:20; lighter regime for large suppliers (2:81) *(secondary)* |
| [**Deferred-debit cards**](provisions/deferred-debit-cards.md) | *Current:* out of scope. **CCD2:** Member-State option to exempt | NL does not exempt but applies the rules **6 months later** (new Wft 1:20(4)) |

## Responsible lending

| Provision | Current law → CCD2 directive | Dutch implementation (36924 / AMvB) |
|---|---|---|
| [**Creditworthiness**](provisions/creditworthiness.md) | *Current:* Wft 4:34 assessment. **CCD2 (art 18):** "necessary and proportionate"; bans social-network data and special-category data | Wft 4:34 recast; **new Wft 4:34a** bans social-network & special-category data; 4:34a(2) basis to redact special-category "bijvangst"; AMvB removes €1,000 verification & €250 BKR thresholds |
| [**Ban on credit to minors**](provisions/minors-ban.md) | *Current:* no specific ban. **CCD2 (art 6):** objective-justified age differentiation permitted | **New Wft 4:34b** public-law ban + mandatory age verification; **BW 7:75(4)** makes agreements with minors *voidable* |
| [**Arrears & forbearance**](provisions/arrears-forbearance.md) | *Current:* Wft 4:35a + BW 70a/128aa (NPL-directive). **CCD2 (arts 35-36):** inform of arrears; reasonable forbearance | New **Wft 4:35b** (early detection + referral to debt advice) and **BW 7:68a** (referral duty) |

## Conduct & information

| Provision | Current law → CCD2 directive | Dutch implementation (36924 / AMvB) |
|---|---|---|
| [**Advertising**](provisions/advertising.md) | *Current:* general standard-information rules. **CCD2 (art 8):** ban on misleading credit ads | Ban on ads suggesting credit improves the consumer's finances or that a BKR registration has little effect; **four new BGfo advertising prohibitions** (AMvB) |
| [**Pre-contractual / withdrawal / early repayment**](provisions/precontractual.md) *(stub)* | **CCD2 (arts 10-21):** SECCI modernised; 14-day withdrawal; stronger early-repayment cost reduction | Implemented across Wft / BW Boek 7; tying prohibited in principle (BW 7:77) except insurance / payment-or-savings account |
