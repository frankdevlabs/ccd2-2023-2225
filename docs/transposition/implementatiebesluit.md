# Implementatiebesluit (AMvB) — the lower-level Dutch rules

> The order in council that fills in the act. Tracked separately from the wet
> ([`nl-pipeline.md`](nl-pipeline.md)) because it has its own consultation and Staatsblad track, though it
> is intended to enter into force **together with** the wet.

## Identification

- **Instrument:** Besluit tot wijziging van het Besluit Gedragstoezicht financiële ondernemingen Wft
  (BGfo), het Besluit kredietvergoeding en het Besluit bestuurlijke boetes financiële sector
  (Implementatiebesluit herziene richtlijn consumentenkrediet).
- **KetenID:** WGK026387 — [wetgevingskalender](https://wetgevingskalender.overheid.nl/Regeling/WGK026387)
- **Internetconsultatie:** opened **22 December 2025**, closed **2 February 2026** —
  [internetconsultatie](https://www.internetconsultatie.nl/implementatiebesluitrichtlijnconsumentenkrediet/b1)
  (accompanied by a separate consultation on the provisiegebod for consumer credit).
- **Status (13 Jun 2026):** not yet finalised / not in the Staatsblad. Not required to be laid before the
  Tweede Kamer.

## Key contents

- **Open-norm creditworthiness assessment**; abolition of the **€1,000 verification threshold** (open
  verification norm, art 113 BGfo).
- Removal of the **€250 BKR-consultation threshold** so BKR must be consulted for **every** consumer-credit
  agreement (art 114 BGfo).
- **Annual re-assessment** for revolving credit (doorlopend krediet).
- A specific **cost regime / cap for deferred-payment credit** (BNPL and cards), including a maximum
  late-payment fee (new art 12 Besluit kredietvergoeding — chargeable 14 days after notice of default,
  **max €20 per notice and €60 per calendar year**, per Loyens & Loeff — *secondary, verify*).
- New free-of-charge general-information duties; **four new advertising prohibitions** (BGfo) — see
  [`../provisions/advertising.md`](../provisions/advertising.md).

> Figures marked *secondary* are from law-firm briefings cross-checked against the draft AMvB; verify
> against the final text once published in the Staatsblad.
