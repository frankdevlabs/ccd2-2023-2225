# Deferred-debit cards — 6-month-later application

> **Directive:** CCD2 Member-State option to exempt deferred-debit cards. **Dutch implementation:** new
> Wft 1:20(4). Operative text (when transcribed):
> [`extracts/transposition-nl/36924_final-provisions.md`](../../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_final-provisions.md).

## What CCD2 allows
CCD2 permits Member States to **exempt deferred-debit cards** (a Member-State option).

## How the Netherlands implements it
The Netherlands chose **not** to exempt them, but applies the rules to deferred-debit cards (creditcards)
**6 months after entry into force** of the Dutch act (new **art 1:20(4) Wft**). Because entry into force
is by koninklijk besluit and can differ per article, this staggered switch-on is mechanically possible.

## Stakeholder note
A joint **Betaalvereniging Nederland / NVB** response in the Implementatiebesluit consultation (2 Feb 2026)
engages with the deferred-debit-card treatment — see [`../stakeholders.md`](../stakeholders.md).
