## Headline points (from the dossier)
- **SECCI-style** pre-contractual information modernised.
- **Right of withdrawal (14 days)** maintained.
- **Early-repayment cost reduction** strengthened.
- **Tying prohibited** in principle (BW 7:77 samenloop) except insurance / a payment-or-savings account.
- Advisory standards; AFM as supervisor with product-intervention powers (Wft 1:77q).
- Crowdfunding credit and lease/rental with a purchase option/intention brought into Wft scope; ongoing
  consultation on regulating private lease without a purchase option; provisiegebod consultation; APR /
  maximale kredietvergoeding handled via the Besluit kredietvergoeding.
