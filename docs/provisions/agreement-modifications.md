# Modifications & changes in the borrowing rate

> **Directive:** CCD2 **Arts 22–23** —
> [`agreement-form-modifications.md#article-22`](../../extracts/directive/agreement-form-modifications.md#article-22).
> **Dutch implementation:** **BW 7:62** ([`#bw-7-62`](../../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-II-bw7.md#bw-7-62)).

## What CCD2 requires
- **Art 22** — before modifying the agreement the creditor must give, on a durable medium, a clear
  description of the changes, the timescale, the **complaint route, the time to complain and the competent
  authority**.
- **Art 23** — any change in the borrowing rate must be notified **in good time before it takes effect**,
  with the new payment amounts; periodic information is allowed only where the change tracks a publicly
  available reference rate.

## How the Netherlands implements it
- **BW 7:62** is amended for Art 23: the consumer must be informed **"geruime tijd"** before a debetrente
  change; the periodic-information derogation applies only where the change follows a reference rate that
  is publicly available and personally communicated with the new instalment amounts.
- The Art 22 modification-information duties are implemented across the BW Boek 7 conduct provisions; NL
  detail here is lighter than the headline provisions — expand from the MvT (36924-3) as needed.

## Sourcing
Voorstel van wet + MvT (36924-2/3).
