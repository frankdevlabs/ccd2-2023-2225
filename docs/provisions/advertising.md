# Advertising restrictions

> **Directive:** CCD2 **Arts 7–8** — [`precontractual-information.md#article-7`](../../extracts/directive/precontractual-information.md#article-7).
> **Dutch implementation:** **BW 7:59** ([`#bw-7-59`](../../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-II-bw7.md#bw-7-59))
> + four new BGfo prohibitions via the AMvB
> ([`#bgfo-advertising`](../../extracts/transposition-nl/amvb_implementatiebesluit.md#bgfo-advertising)).

## What CCD2 requires
- **Art 7** — advertising and marketing of credit must be **fair, clear and not misleading**; no wording
  may create false expectations about availability or cost.
- **Art 8** — where an interest rate or cost figure is shown, **standard information** (APR, total amount,
  etc.) must be included, presented clearly with a representative example.

## How the Netherlands implements it
- **BW 7:59** is recast: misleading credit advertising is an **oneerlijke handelspraktijk** (Boek 6 art
  193b); ads must carry a **clear "lenen kost geld" warning** and the Art 8(3)–(6) standard information
  where a rate or cost figure is shown.
- A ban on advertising that a **BKR registration** has little or no effect on a credit application.
- **Four new advertising prohibitions** are set out in the BGfo via the Implementatiebesluit — see
  [`../transposition/implementatiebesluit.md`](../transposition/implementatiebesluit.md).

## Sourcing

verified against the final AMvB text.
