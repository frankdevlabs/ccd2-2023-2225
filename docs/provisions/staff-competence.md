# Knowledge & competence requirements for staff

> **Directive:** CCD2 **Art 33** —
> [`apr-conduct.md#article-33`](../../extracts/directive/apr-conduct.md#article-33).
> **Dutch implementation:** the Wft / BGfo **vakbekwaamheid** regime
> ([`36924_artikel-I-wft.md`](../../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-I-wft.md)).

## What CCD2 requires
- Creditors and intermediaries must ensure staff **possess and keep up to date an appropriate level of
  knowledge and competence** for manufacturing, offering and granting credit, intermediation and advice,
  including on consumer rights and any ancillary services (Art 33(1)).
- Member States must set **minimum knowledge and competence requirements** and ensure the competent
  authority supervises compliance (Art 33(2)–(3)).

## How the Netherlands implements it
- Delivered through the existing **Wft vakbekwaamheid** framework (BGfo Deel vakbekwaamheid / Wft 4:9),
  with AFM as supervisor; the bill brings the newly in-scope credit providers within it.
- NL detail here is lighter than the headline provisions — expand from the MvT (36924-3) as needed.
