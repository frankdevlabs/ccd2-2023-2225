# Overdraft facilities & overrunning

> **Directive:** CCD2 **Arts 24–25** —
> [`overdraft-withdrawal-repayment.md#article-24`](../../extracts/directive/overdraft-withdrawal-repayment.md#article-24).
> **Dutch implementation:** **BW 7:63** (overdraft) ([`#bw-7-63`](../../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-II-bw7.md#bw-7-63))
> + **BW 7:70** (overrunning) ([`#bw-7-70`](../../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-II-bw7.md#bw-7-70)).

## What CCD2 requires
- **Art 24 (authorised overdraft):** at least **monthly statements**; advance notice of rate/charge
  increases; **30 days' notice** of any reduction/cancellation; and, before enforcement, the right to repay
  the drawn amount in **12 equal monthly instalments**.
- **Art 25 (overrunning):** disclose the possibility and its cost in the account agreement; on a
  significant overrun exceeding one month, inform the consumer without delay and, on regular overrunning,
  **offer advice and refer to debt-advisory services**; 30-day notice and the 12-instalment repayment right
  apply as for overdrafts.

## How the Netherlands implements it
- **BW 7:63** (overdraft): "regelmatig" becomes **"ten minste maandelijks"**; advance "geruime tijd"
  notice; new leden 4–5 add the **30-day notice** and **12-instalment** repayment right on reduction or
  cancellation.
- **BW 7:70** (overrunning): aligns the cross-reference to **Art 25** of the directive, adds the repayment
  date to the information set, and adds leden 3–5: **offer advice / refer to debt-advisory services**,
  30-day notice and the 12-instalment repayment right.

Voorstel van wet + MvT (36924-2/3).
