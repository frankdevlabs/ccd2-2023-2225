# Form & content of the credit agreement

> **Directive:** CCD2 **Arts 20–21** —
> [`agreement-form-modifications.md#article-20`](../../extracts/directive/agreement-form-modifications.md#article-20).
> **Dutch implementation:** **BW 7:61** ([`#bw-7-61`](../../extracts/transposition-nl/36924_artikel-II-bw7.md#bw-7-61)).

## What CCD2 requires
- The agreement and any modification must be on **paper or a durable medium**, with a copy for every party
  (Art 20).
- Art 21 sets the **mandatory content list** (a–x): type of credit, parties' full contact details, total
  amount, duration, borrowing rate, APR and total payable, payment schedule, late-payment rate and a
  **warning on the consequences of missing payments**, withdrawal right, early-repayment right, the
  competent authority, and — new — the **contact details of debt-advisory services**.

## How the Netherlands implements it
- **BW 7:61** is recast: durable-medium + copy rule; the content list is updated to add **telephone and
  email** of the parties (onderdeel b), the **arrears warning** (onderdeel n), the durable-medium choice
  (onderdeel q/y) and the new **onderdeel x** — debt-advisory contact details and a recommendation to
  contact them on repayment difficulty. Information must be "duidelijk leesbaar" and channel-appropriate.

## Sourcing
Voorstel van wet + MvT (36924-2/3).
