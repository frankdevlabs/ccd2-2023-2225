## What CCD2 requires
- The APR (jaarlijks kostenpercentage) is computed with the **Annex III formula**, equating the present
  value of all drawdowns, repayments and charges (Art 30(1)).
- The **total cost of credit** is determined per Art 30(2), including account/payment-instrument costs
  unless the account is optional and separately identified; fixed-rate assumptions apply where terms are
  unquantifiable (Art 30(3)–(4)). The Commission may amend the assumptions by delegated act (Art 30(5)).

## How the Netherlands implements it
- The APR formula and cost components are set out in the **BGfo / Besluit kredietvergoeding** (the
  AMvB level), aligned to Annex III.
- **BW 7:71** updates the internal cross-reference from "artikel 19" to **"artikel 30"** of the directive.
