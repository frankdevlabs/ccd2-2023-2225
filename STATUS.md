## One-line status

CCD2 is **adopted and in force** but the Netherlands **missed the 20 Nov 2025 transposition deadline** and
received a **Commission letter of formal notice on 30 Jan 2026**; the implementing bill (**36924**) is in
Tweede Kamer written preparation (verslag 36924-6, 21 May 2026; the government's *nota naar aanleiding van
het verslag*, 36924-8, followed on 23 Jun 2026 — awaiting plenary scheduling), the accompanying AMvB has
been consulted but not finalised, and the target is
entry into force aligned to the **20 Nov 2026** application date — "under pressure" per the sector.

### A. EU instrument

#### Directive (EU) 2023/2225 — the act
- **Stage:** Adopted (18 Oct 2023), in force (19 Nov 2023). EU legislative process **complete**.
- **Latest act:** Publication in OJ L, 2023/2225 (30 Oct 2023) — [digest](docs/directive.md) · [EUR-Lex](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2023/2225/oj)
- **Owner:** Member States (transposition); European Commission (enforcement).
- **Position:** Maximum harmonisation; abolishes the €200 lower limit, raises the ceiling to €100,000, brings BNPL / cards / overdrafts into scope, recasts the creditworthiness test, bans credit to minors.
- **Next:** **Application from 20 Nov 2026**; CCD1 repealed from that date (transitional regime for existing open-end agreements).

#### European Commission — enforcer
- **Stage:** Infringement opened against NL for non-communication of complete transposition.
- **Latest act:** **Letter of formal notice, 30 Jan 2026** (package INF/26/115) — [details](docs/infringement.md) · [press corner](https://ec.europa.eu/commission/presscorner/detail/en/inf_26_115)
- **Owner:** DG JUST / DG FISMA (consumer-credit file).
- **Position:** NL (one of 23 Member States) failed to communicate complete transposition by 20 Nov 2025.
- **Next:** Possible **reasoned opinion** if the 2-month response (from 30 Jan 2026) is unsatisfactory.

### B. Netherlands transposition

#### Regering / Ministerie van Financiën — owner of the bill + AMvB
- **Stage:** Bill submitted to the Tweede Kamer (1 Apr 2026); government reply to the verslag delivered (nota, 23 Jun 2026).
- **Latest act:** Nota naar aanleiding van het verslag (**36924-8, 23 Jun 2026**) — [pipeline](docs/transposition/nl-pipeline.md) · [TK download](https://www.tweedekamer.nl/downloads/document?id=2026D31985)
- **Owner:** Minister van Financiën **E. (Eelco) Heinen**; co-signed by the Staatssecretaris van J&V.
- **Position:** Brings BNPL / cards / overdrafts under the Wft; new arts 4:34a/4:34b Wft; deferred-debit cards +6 months. Acknowledges the missed deadline (complexity + many Member-State options).
- **Next:** Defends the bill at the **wetgevingsoverleg 28 Sep 2026** (Gepland), then TK vote.

#### Tweede Kamer
- **Stage:** Schriftelijke voorbereiding (written preparation).
- **Latest act:** Nota naar aanleiding van het verslag (**36924-8, 23 Jun 2026**) — the government's reply to the verslag (36924-6, 21 May 2026); same day as the brief regering reactie op commissieverzoek (36924-7) — [pipeline](docs/transposition/nl-pipeline.md) · [TK download](https://www.tweedekamer.nl/downloads/document?id=2026D31985)
- **Owner:** Vaste commissie voor Financiën.
- **Position:** Committee questions issued (technische briefing 12 May; inbreng 19 May), answered by the nota 23 Jun; rondetafelgesprek (committee hearing) held 25 Jun 2026 (*Uitgevoerd*).
- **Next:** **Wetgevingsoverleg 28 Sep 2026** (legislative debate, *Gepland* — convocatie 2026D35223), then plenary vote.

#### Eerste Kamer
- **Stage:** Not started ("in behandeling bij de Tweede Kamer").
- **Latest act:** — — [EK dossier](https://www.eerstekamer.nl/wetsvoorstel/36924_implementatiewet_herziene)
- **Owner:** —
- **Position:** —
- **Next:** Referral after a Tweede Kamer vote.

#### Implementatiebesluit (AMvB)
- **Stage:** Consulted, not finalised; intended to enter into force together with the wet.
- **Latest act:** Internetconsultatie closed **2 Feb 2026** (opened 22 Dec 2025) — [AMvB digest](docs/transposition/implementatiebesluit.md) · [wetgevingskalender WGK026387](https://wetgevingskalender.overheid.nl/Regeling/WGK026387)
- **Owner:** Ministerie van Financiën.
- **Position:** Open-norm creditworthiness; removes the €1,000 verification & €250 BKR thresholds; late-payment fee cap; four new advertising prohibitions.
- **Next:** Finalisation / Staatsblad publication.

#### AFM — supervisor
- **Stage:** Preparing supervision; accepting early licence applications and publishing CCDII conduct expectations ahead of 20 Nov 2026.
- **Latest act:** Updated CCDII sector-page guidance (both pages refreshed Jul 2026) — overview page now names the Wft licensing/registration hooks (art 2:60 vergunning for BNPL/deferred-debit cards/lease-with-option; art 2:81 registratie for large short-term-BNPL intermediaries; art 2:80 vergunning for other new intermediaries) + an intermediary-registration facility; the "drie doorlopende verplichtingen" sub-page sharpens proportionele invulling for short-term credit (≤3 mths: age + BKR check — refuse on existing BKR arrears; assessment not solely on credit history + verify all information; joint acceptance policy to avoid a "race to the bottom"; retain BNPL gedragscode measures) alongside age verification (iDIN) and early signalling — [AFM context](docs/transposition/supervision-afm.md) · [AFM CCDII page](https://www.afm.nl/nl-nl/sector/kredietaanbieders/herziene-richtlijn-consumentenkrediet-ccdii)
- **Owner:** AFM (conduct supervisor).
- **Position:** Supports lowering the €250 BKR threshold and statutory age verification; treats creditworthiness as an open norm to be specified via sector methodology; licences cannot be granted before the rules are finalised.
- **Next:** Process licence applications so assessments are done before 20 Nov 2026.
